Marrakesh, Morocco

Marrakesh's vibrant colors, aromatic dishes, indecipherable sounds and inimitable feel will linger in your memory long after you leave its corridors. Plan to spend most of your time in or around the medina, Marrakech's fortified old city. Marvel at ancient, elaborately decorated sights like the Saadian Tombs, the Badi Palace and Koutoubia Mosque before testing your haggling skills in Jemaa El Fna. And don't forget to save time for quintessential Moroccan experiences, such as camel rides in the desert and soaks in hammams (public baths).
